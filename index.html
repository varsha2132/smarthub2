Your Skills

Identify and showcase your skills that can help you in your career:

  • 1. Leadership: Describe any leadership roles or experiences you have.
  • 2. Communication: Highlight your communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • 3. Technical: List any technical skills, such as programming languages or software proficiency.
  • 4. Creativity: Mention any creative abilities or hobbies that set you apart.

Your Interests

Share your interests and passions that may guide your career choices:

  • 1. Hobbies: What hobbies or activities do you enjoy outside of your studies?
  • 2. Subjects: Are there any school subjects that particularly interest you?
  • 3. Career Fields: Do you have any specific career fields you're passionate about?
  • 4. Goals: What are your long-term goals and aspirations?

Your Academic Performance
